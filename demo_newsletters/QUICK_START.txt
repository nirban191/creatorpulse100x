⚡ FASTEST WAY TO TEST:

1. Open CreatorPulse in browser: http://localhost:8501

2. Go to "Style Trainer" page (sidebar)

3. Choose ONE of these methods:

   METHOD A - Copy & Paste (Easiest)
   ─────────────────────────────────
   • Open: style_1_casual_tech.txt
   • Copy ALL content (Cmd+A, Cmd+C)
   • Paste into text area
   • Click "Train Writing Style"
   
   METHOD B - Upload Files
   ───────────────────────
   • Click "Upload your past newsletters"
   • Select 2-3 style_*.txt files
   • Upload
   • Click "Train Writing Style"
   
   METHOD C - Quick Test All Styles
   ─────────────────────────────────
   • Open: ALL_STYLES_COMBINED.txt
   • Copy everything
   • Paste into text area
   • This creates a hybrid style!

4. Wait 2-3 seconds for mock training

5. Go to "Generate Newsletter" (sidebar)

6. Click "Generate Newsletter Draft"

7. Compare the output style with your training input!
